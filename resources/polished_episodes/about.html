Welcome to Four Track Friday

In the middle of 2018, Joe B. and I were chatting on the phone. I had a problem. I found myself constantly sending and receiving songs via text. Receive a song, send a song, re-send another song.

I came up with a simple idea: what if I put everyone engaging in this on a single email list? The result was a big, blind-carbon-copy sent email list. That evolved and is now sent to individual users with Python. Then there was a static website, where only the newest episode could be viewed. Now there's this Python-based web app!

If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please email me at fourtrackfriday@icloud.com.
