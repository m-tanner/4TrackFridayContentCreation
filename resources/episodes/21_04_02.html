Hey friends,

Get ready for some great songs and some short descriptions :)

EDM | Simple | Autograf, Victoria Zaro

From one of my favorite shows I've seen in Seattle, Autograf's "Simple" has some of the most turn-you-on guitar sound design out there. The attack is just perfect! It's right at the beginning and will make you want to keep the song on repeat.

Heartfelt Indie | either way | mou

This track is vulnerable to the point of fragility. Curl up with it and "feel [your] breath 'til the next one comes."

Dub Disco | Early Night | COFRESI

The opener for Autograf on that legendary night (how rare is it that the opener is amazing?!), I always associate the two acts together. Delivering dub, bass, samples, and fun production, this should fire you up for just about anything!

Echo Pop | Bones | Telenova

Finally, we have a track that's perfectly paradoxical.

Kill me softly, make me scream
Call me crazy, set me free
Everything I'll ever need, say you'll die for me

Enjoy!
