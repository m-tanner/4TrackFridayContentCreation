After hearing a few of Yi-Lynn's pieces now it's clear that one of her strengths is in the little details. The way her vocal quivers ever so slightly on main lyric or how she fills the minutia of the song's story with turns of phrase. I especially liked how she sings "Caroline, quickly now, wind back the tape" and the guitar (?) plays in reverse. Clever.
